Noodles

Description

This homemade sauce makes a sweet and spicy foil for linguine. The best sesame noodles I have every had!

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package linguine pasta
  • 6 cloves garlic, minced
  • 6 tablespoons sugar
  • 6 tablespoons safflower oil
  • 6 tablespoons rice vinegar
  • 6 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 2 tablespoons sesame oil
  • 2 teaspoons chili sauce
  • 6 green onions, sliced
  • 1 teaspoon sesame seeds, toasted

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of lightly salted water to boil.
  2. Add pasta, and cook until al dente, about 8 to 10 minutes.
  3. Drain, and transfer to a serving bowl.
  4. Meanwhile, place a saucepan over medium-high heat.
  5. Stir in garlic, sugar, oil, vinegar, soy sauce, sesame oil, and chili sauce.
  6. Bring to a boil, stirring constantly, until sugar dissolves.
  7. Pour sauce over linguine, and toss to coat.
  8. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
